WHAT IS CYBERSECURITY?

  • Protection of Sensitive Information: Cybersecurity measures help in protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access and theft. This information could include personal data, financial details, intellectual property, or classified government information. Could also talk about identity theft
  • Prevention of Data Breaches: Cybersecurity measures safeguard organizations from data breaches that can lead to significant financial losses, damage to reputation, and legal consequences. Breaches can occur due to hacking, phishing attacks, or other malicious activities.

HOW DOES IT RELATE TO CYBERBULLYING?

Cybersecurity and cyberbullying prevention assist with online safety and raising awareness in educational atmospheres

In an educational realm, awareness would inherently help people recognize signs of online harassment, how to prevent it, and what to do when faced with that situation

Some solutions involve secure online behavior, strong passwords, and proper security practices which have proven to reduce an individual's risk of falling victim to cyberbullying
